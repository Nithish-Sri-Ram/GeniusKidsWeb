About Genius Kids

We are..

Genius kids is an International and contemporary Montessori school. Which strives for a balance of academic self-direction. Here we concern for the child and a strong value system. Genius kids dedicates itself in providing an authentic Montessori education to children from birth to 18 years of age. We provide alternate education for children aiding development experiential.

The values of excellence, respect, compassion, peace and community inspire our school's rich tradition of nurturing each child's natural love of learning.

In genius kids we focus on the child- working on his/her strength and giving time to scale up academics at their pace.

We provide study circles for families that create a democratic platform for parents and teachers to discuss freely related to educational philosophy.

Our Mission

Educate, Empower and Encourage the young minds to Experience the Excellence by developing the core values like Integrity, courage, conformity, self reliance and responsibility through healthy practices in the bliss full approach.

Our Vision

To impart the quality education for the holistic development of a child by fostering physical, emotional, spiritual, and social growth in them.

"Genius kids International play school was awarded as the best Proprietary preschool by Education World India Preschool Rankings 2018-19 at the 9th Education National Conference 2019 held in Bangalore"
